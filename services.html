How it works

Simple repair process

Clients know what happens before handing over a phone, laptop or PC.

1

WhatsApp first

Send your device model, problem, and pictures or a short video.

2

Problem check

I ask quick questions to understand what happened before the fault started.

3

Quick diagnosis

You get a clear explanation of the likely cause and next step.
