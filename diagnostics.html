What a diagnostic check can include

This gives your service a more professional feel and helps prevent confusion.

Fault description

What the device is doing and when the problem happens.

Possible cause

Likely reason, such as software, battery, screen, storage, RAM, charging or board issue.

Recommended fix

Next best step, including repair, replacement, cleanup, setup or further testing.

Cost estimate

Expected labour and whether parts may be needed.

Risk note

For sensitive jobs, clients are told what can and cannot be guaranteed.

Test result

What was tested after the repair or setup was completed.
