What affects the price?

Price depends on the real fault

A slow laptop and a damaged motherboard are not the same job. This explains the price difference before clients book.

Time needed

Some jobs are quick checks, others need deeper testing or installation time.

Parts needed

Batteries, screens, SSDs, RAM and other parts are separate from labour.

Risk and complexity

Component-level or liquid damage work can need more careful handling.
